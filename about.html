About EABMTI

Founded in 2009 by Rosemary Mwakitwange (MBA) and Adeodatus Kakorozya (Graphics and Design), to address the gap in the press rooms of lack of journalistic knowledge and acumen. Inspired by their successful turnaround of one of the biggest print media house in East Africa at that time, the New Habari (2006) Limited company, one of the major print media houses at that time, the trio, stepped out form that company to start a training incubator to breed a new generation of young and skilled Journalists to feed into the sector.

Vision

We envision to make EABMTI training solution, a dynamic, vibrant, value based, learning organization comprising of exceptionally trained, experienced, motivated and committed professionals, who facilitate seamless transformation of our trainees to excellent performers. Our vision is to become the company for supporting and sustaining Tanzania human resource capital for employers and businesses, in the content of Tanzania for Tanzanians.

Mission

We are on a mission to train and build capacity of young and other Tanzanians to develop and grow their career for employment in private sector, Not for profit organizations as well as self-employment for wealth creation.

We plan to achieve this mission by:

Identifying and training a new generation of watchdog journalists on investigative reporting and media.

Facilitating young people who wish to study for Masters and Phd. This include training on how to write an abstract, concept note, develop research questions/hypothesis and research methodology. We also facilitate development of thesis and publications.

Providing skill based training on entrepreneurship and business development.

Help new businesses and individuals transform their business ideas into full business proposals.

Provide career guidance and counselling for new employees, promotion seekers.

Providing skill based training on entrepreneurship and business development.

Our Values

EABMTI is guided by the following values acronym as three Cs namely Competence, Confidence and Competitiveness

Competence

Ability to do something well, specifically acquisition of specific skills needed to do a specific job well. We at EABMTI uses competence based curriculum designed to impart you with the skills to perform specific assignments or jobs well beyond expectations. Our curriculum is about practical skills beyond theories and academics. Our training is done by doing, in very practical terms.

Confidence

Our curriculum is specifically designed to build confidence of the trainees to deliver and share their story with others what they do, how they do and why they perform in such a high level. We believe that no matter how good you are, if you are not able to tell your story in an effective manner, nobody will know about it.

Competitiveness

We at EABMTI have proven over the last 9 years or our existence that our programs increase with certainty the capacity of our graduates to delivery above the standard quality, and compete with merits in the local and International markets.
