EABMTI's Incubator

Changing lives, Changing Nations by turning your failure into a testimony!

We are a Tanzania registered business and training incubator. We are offering skills for young people so that they can be employed, or create their own jobs and employ others. Our school is a solution to the problem of employment among young people in Tanzania. We are concerned with a large number of secondary school failure, at least in the last three years the form four failure rate averages about 70%. This means about eight hundred thousand (800,000) young Tanzanians who complete secondary school in Tanzania, fail to acquire the minimum level to qualify for high school education. This number is similar to twelve times the size of the National Stadium, every year.

We are contributing to absorption of unemployment by providing skills that can make youth employable, and remove them from the streets. We are a business incubator, training young Tanzanians on employable skills ranging from business, entrepreneurship, sales, trading, communication, farming, value addition, value chain etc.

Registration

You can register with us at our Tegeta Masaiti Center, or call Mama Rosemary, founder and lead trainer, directly to organize a counselling session to guide your decision. This process will then be followed by an agreement of your desire course, and therefore registration.

Form Four Graduates with division four and zero pass marks

We are recruiting form four graduates who wish to repeat and make better their passes for business studies of Commerce, Book Keeping and Accounts. This one year review class is mainly organized for those who did not qualify to continue with business studies, and do not want to change their career vision. Our center is the only platform for aspiring business students, to re-focus and remain true in their original career vision. Our typical recruit will be those who have pass marks for social studies, but need business studies qualification to pursue further studies in Business and commercial studies.

Their program will include one year of classroom hours on the three subject areas, through series of attempts and perfection of the past papers for the last 10 years.
This is a one year course.

Procurement Qualification – any two ‘D’s pass mark in any subject

The center will have a class for professional training to prepare for board examinations. This class will be mainly of young form four graduates with two pass marks in any of the subject, not necessarily business. The course is open to form four graduates of this and previous years who wish to build their career in procurement, logistics and value chain. This course is for two years, and involves attending tuition classes either day or evening sessions.

Mega Schools learning Platform for Academic and Professional Qualifications

In partnership with Dr. Orleve Levy Nissenbaum, a Professor of Science from Israel, our center has introduce a learning platform for teachers and students via social media. The platform enables students to connect and take their studies on line, while connecting with the trainer directly. This platform has been successfully used in Israel and Turkey, and will be piloted here in Tanzania, through our programs. The Mega platform is a perfect tool for those preparing for secondary, high school and professional training on business and management.
